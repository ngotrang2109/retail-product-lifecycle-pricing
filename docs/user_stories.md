# User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

## US1: Product Lifecycle Classification
As a **Retail Operation Manager**  
I want **products to be automatically classified into lifecycle stages**  
So that **I can apply appropriate pricing and inventory strategies.**

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Products are classified as New, Growth, Maturity, or Decline
- Classification rules are configurable
- Lifecycle stage is visible to business users

---

## US2: Pricing Strategy Recommendation
As a **Sales and Marketing Manager**  
I want **pricing strategies recommended by lifecycle stage and product role**  
So that **pricing decisions are consistent and scalable.**

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Pricing strategy is assigned based on lifecycle and role
- Strategy rationale is documented
- Manual override is allowed

---

## US3: Inventory Risk Identification
As an **Production Operation Manager**  
I want **to identify overstock and stockout risks**  
**So that **I can take preventive actions.**

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Overstock and stockout risks are clearly flagged
- Risk logic is transparent
- Alerts are triggered for high-risk products

---

## US4: Demand Forecasting Support
As an **Production Operation Manager**  
I want **demand forecasts for restock planning**  
So that **inventory levels align with expected demand.**

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Forecast is generated for defined time horizon
- Forecast is based on historical sales
- Forecast is updated periodically

---

## US5: Workflow Automation
As a **Retail Operation Manager**  
I want **restock workflows to be triggered automatically**  
So that **actions are taken without manual delays.**

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Workflow is triggered based on defined rules
- Responsible teams are notified
- Actions are logged for tracking
