# User Stories

## 1. Purpose
This document captures key user stories that describe how business users interact with lifecycle, pricing, inventory, and demand signals generated by the analytics solution.

The stories focus on business decisions and outcomes, not system implementation.

---

## 2. Scope
The user stories cover:
- Product lifecycle classification
- Pricing strategy review
- Inventory risk detection
- Demand forecasting for restock planning
- Workflow-triggered operational reviews

---
## 3. User Stories by Functional Requirement

### US-01: Product Lifecycle Visibility
(FR1 – Lifecycle Classification)

**As a** Prodution Operations Manager
**I want to** see the lifecycle stage of each SKU over time
**So that** I can understand product maturity and adjust assortment decisions accordingly.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Lifecycle stage is available at SKU level.
- Lifecycle can change over time based on sales performance.
- Lifecycle status is explainable and rule-based.

Data Source:
- dm_daily_sku
- dm_sku_snapshot

---

### US-02: Pricing Strategy Recommendation
(FR2 – Pricing Strategy Recommendation)

**As a** Sales Manager
**I want** pricing recommendations aligned with product lifecycle stage
**So that** pricing decisions are consistent and data-driven.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Pricing strategy is derived from lifecycle stage and sales performance
- Recommendations are advisory, not automated
- Business users can override recommendations if needed

Data Source
- dm_sku_snapshot

---

### US-03: Key & Potential Product Identification

(FR4 – Product Prioritization)

**As a** Retail Operations Manager
**I want** products classified as Key or Potential
**So that** I can prioritize inventory, pricing, and marketing investments.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Key products show stable, high sales contribution
- Potential products show positive growth signals
- Classification is explainable and reviewed periodically

Data Source:
- dm_sku_snapshot
- Lifecycle & Pricing Rules

---

###US-04: Inventory Risk Monitoring

(FR3 – Inventory Risk Identification)

**As a** Production Operations Manager
**I want** early visibility into overstock and stockout risks
**So that** I can take timely inventory actions.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Overstock risk: low sales velocity + high inventory
- Stockout risk: high sales velocity + low inventory
- Signals are available before inventory reaches critical levels

Data Source:
- dm_daily_sku
- dm_sku_snapshot

---

###US-05: Demand Forecasting for Restock Planning

(FR5 – Demand Forecasting)

**As a** Production Operations Manager
**I want** demand trend indicators and expected inventory coverage
**So that** I can plan restock quantity and timing proactively.

Acceptance Criteria
- Average daily sales is calculated using historical sales
- Days on Hand / stockout risk indicators are available
- Forecast outputs support decision-making, not automatic ordering

Data Source
- dm_daily_sku
- dm_sku_snapshot

---

### US-06: Workflow Trigger for Restock Review

(FR6 – Workflow Automation)

**As a** Production Operations Manager
**I want** restock review workflows triggered for high-risk SKUs
**So that** stockout risks are addressed before sales are lost.

Acceptance Criteria
- Trigger based on lifecycle stage, product role, and inventory risk
- Workflow includes clear owner and expected outcome
- Final decision remains manual

Data Source
- dm_sku_snapshot
- Workflow Scenarios
